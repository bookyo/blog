## 1. Video to MP3 — Extract Audio in One Click

Got a video file and need just the audio? **Video to MP3** strips the audio track and encodes it to MP3 with configurable bitrate (128–320 kbps), sample rate, and channel options.

**Use it for:** Extracting podcast audio from video recordings, pulling music from concert footage, or converting lecture recordings into listenable audio files.

→ [Try Video to MP3](https://elysiatools.com/en/tools/video-to-mp3)

## 2. Video to WAV — Lossless Audio Extraction

When quality matters more than file size, reach for **Video to WAV**. It extracts audio without any lossy compression — perfect for audio engineers, musicians, or anyone who needs the exact original signal.

**Use it for:** Archiving audio at full fidelity, preparing source material for audio processing pipelines, or extracting clean audio from screen recordings.

→ [Try Video to WAV](https://elysiatools.com/en/tools/video-to-wav)

## 3. Video to Opus — Web-Optimized Audio

Opus is the codec that powers WhatsApp voice messages, WebRTC calls, and most modern web audio. **Video to Opus** converts video audio to Opus format with bitrates from 96–256 kbps, making it ideal for any web-adjacent audio project.

**Use it for:** Preparing audio assets for web apps, reducing bandwidth in audio streaming projects, or converting video podcasts into web-player-friendly files.

→ [Try Video to Opus](https://elysiatools.com/en/tools/video-to-opus)

---

## 4. Video Stream Extractor — Split Audio and Video Without Re-encoding

Why re-encode when you just need to separate streams? **Video Stream Extractor** uses stream copy (not transcoding) to extract audio, video, or both simultaneously. It handles 500MB+ files and supports all major video formats.

This means the extraction is instantaneous — no quality loss, no waiting. If you need both streams, it bundles them into a ZIP automatically.

**Use it for:** Pulling audio tracks from MKV files, extracting clean video without audio for editing, or preparing separate assets for a video editing pipeline.

→ [Try Video Stream Extractor](https://elysiatools.com/en/tools/video-stream-extractor)

## 5. MP4 to GIF — Turn Clips into Shareable Animations

GIFs are everywhere in documentation, product demos, and social sharing. **MP4 to GIF** converts video clips (up to 30 seconds) into optimized GIFs with palette optimization and Bayer dithering for smooth gradients.

You control output dimensions, frame rate (1–30 fps), color palette size (2–256 colors), and quality settings. The tool automatically generates an optimized color palette to minimize banding in large flat-color areas.

**Use it for:** Creating product demo GIFs for README files, making bug reproduction animations for GitHub issues, or generating shareable clips for marketing.

→ [Try MP4 to GIF](https://elysiatools.com/en/tools/mp4-to-gif)

## 6. GIF to MP4 — Convert Animations to Proper Video

GIF files are bulky and don't support alpha transparency in most players. **GIF to MP4** converts animated GIFs into MP4 files with H.264, H.265, or VP9 codecs — at resolutions up to 4K.

You can choose quality presets (low/medium/high/ultra), apply custom backgrounds for transparency, control frame rate, and even preserve or remove looping behavior.

**Use it for:** Converting animated assets to video for video editing software, reducing file size for web delivery, or preparing GIF assets for platforms that require MP4 (Twitter/X, Instagram, LinkedIn).

→ [Try GIF to MP4](https://elysiatools.com/en/tools/gif-to-mp4)

## 7. GIF to WebM — Modern Web Video Format

WebM with VP9 codec delivers significantly better compression than H.264, which means smaller file sizes and faster load times. **GIF to WebM** converts animated GIFs to WebM with configurable quality, resolution, and codec options (VP9, VP8, or AV1).

The tool also supports transparency preservation and DASH optimization for streaming, making the output genuinely web-production-ready.

**Use it for:** Replacing GIF-heavy web pages with lighter WebM embeds (WebM files are often 3–10× smaller than equivalent GIFs), preparing assets for web animation projects, or creating preview clips for 3D renders.

→ [Try GIF to WebM](https://elysiatools.com/en/tools/gif-to-webm)
