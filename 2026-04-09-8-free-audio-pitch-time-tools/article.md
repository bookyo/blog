# 8 Free Audio Pitch & Time Tools That Run Entirely in Your Browser

Audio production used to require a DAW, plugins, and hours of setup. Not anymore.

These 8 browser-based tools handle pitch correction, tempo adjustment, time stretching, and EQ — no downloads, no sign-up, no watermark. Drop in a file, tweak a parameter, download the result.

---

## 1. Audio Pitch Up

Raise the pitch of any audio clip without touching the tempo. Drop a vocals file, shift it up a semitone or two, and layer it as a harmony. This means you can build chorus effects from a single recorded take — no second vocalist needed.

**Use it for:** Vocal stacking, key correction, creative pitch effects  
**Link:** [https://elysiatools.com/en/tools/audio-pitch-up](https://elysiatools.com/en/tools/audio-pitch-up)

---

## 2. Audio Pitch Down

The inverse of the above. Drop pitch for a deeper, darker tone. producers use this to thicken bass lines or create a "spooky" voice effect for podcasts. In other words, it's pitch correction in both directions.

**Use it for:** Deepening vocals, bass reinforcement, horror/atmospheric sound design  
**Link:** [https://elysiatools.com/en/tools/audio-pitch-down](https://elysiatools.com/en/tools/audio-pitch-down)

---

## 3. Audio Set Pitch

Not just up or down — set to an exact pitch target. Input a semitone value or frequency in Hz and the tool nails it precisely. This means you can retune a sample to match your project's key without pitch-shifting artifacts.

**Use it for:** Sample key matching, musical project alignment, A/B comparison tuning  
**Link:** [https://elysiatools.com/en/tools/audio-set-pitch](https://elysiatools.com/en/tools/audio-set-pitch)

---

## 4. Audio Set Speed

Change playback speed without altering pitch. Speed up a podcast to 1.5x for faster consumption, or slow it to 0.75x for a dramatic effect. The pitch stays locked. This means voice character, timbre, and formants remain intact — only duration changes.

**Use it for:** Podcast pacing, language learning playback, dramatic slow-downs  
**Link:** [https://elysiatools.com/en/tools/audio-set-speed](https://elysiatools.com/en/tools/audio-set-speed)

---

## 5. Audio Tempo Change

Adjust the tempo (BPM) of a track without affecting pitch. This is the inverse of Set Speed in practice — you input a target BPM and the tool calculates the right rate. Musicians use this to match a sample's groove to their project's beat.

**Use it for:** Beat matching samples, remixing, adjusting loop lengths  
**Link:** [https://elysiatools.com/en/tools/audio-tempo-change](https://elysiatools.com/en/tools/audio-tempo-change)

---

## 6. Audio Time Stretch

Compress or stretch audio to an exact duration — not by BPM, but by seconds. Input "make this 30-second clip exactly 45 seconds" and it handles the math and processing. This means you can fit audio to video timelines without manual calculations.

**Use it for:** Video editing, social media clips, synchronizing multiple audio files  
**Link:** [https://elysiatools.com/en/tools/audio-time-stretch](https://elysiatools.com/en/tools/audio-time-stretch)

---

## 7. Audio Equalizer

A multi-band EQ running in the browser. Boost or cut specific frequency ranges to shape tone. A bass guitar too muddy? Cut around 200-400 Hz. Vocal harshness? Sweep the 3-5 kHz range. This means you can do surgical mixing corrections without launching a full DAW.

**Use it for:** Tone shaping, noise reduction by frequency, mix correction  
**Link:** [https://elysiatools.com/en/tools/audio-equalizer](https://elysiatools.com/en/tools/audio-equalizer)

---

## 8. Audio Bass Boost

Amplify the low-end frequencies of any track. Perfect for adding weight to a thin-sounding track, emphasizing a kick drum, or giving electronic music that chest-pounding sub impact. This means you can hear exactly how the low-end will sit in a mix before committing in your DAW.

**Use it for:** Bass reinforcement, club music prep, checking low-end balance  
**Link:** [https://elysiatools.com/en/tools/audio-bass-boost](https://elysiatools.com/en/tools/audio-bass-boost)

---

## The Unfinished Problem

These 8 tools handle pitch, tempo, time, and EQ — but what about **formant preservation**? When you pitch-shift vocals dramatically, the character of the voice shifts too. Autotune-style formants keep the vocal identity intact. That's still a gap in most browser-based toolchains. Until then, these 8 tools cover 90% of what producers need in a browser tab.
