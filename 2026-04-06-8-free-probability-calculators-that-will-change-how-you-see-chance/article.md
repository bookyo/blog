## The Pattern Nobody Talks About

Here's what these tools reveal when you use them together:

**Your intuition systematically underestimates rare events.** A 1% chance feels like "basically never" to most people. But run 100 independent 1% events, and your probability of at least one success jumps to 63.4%. Suddenly "basically never" looks different.

**Card and dice probabilities are always against you** — but knowing exactly how against you changes how you play. The Royal Flush is 0.000154%. That knowledge doesn't make you stop playing, but it should make you stop counting on it.

**Combinatorics is the hidden language of fairness.** Committee selection, lottery odds, tournament seeding — they're all combinatorics problems. Once you can calculate them, a lot of "random" processes start looking suspiciously like they favor specific outcomes.

## 8. Benford's Law Visualizer

Suspicious of financial data? Benford's Law says the first digit of naturally occurring numbers follows a specific distribution (1 appears as first digit ~30.1% of the time, 9 appears only ~4.6%). If your dataset violates this, something's off.
