## 2. BMR Calculator
**https://elysiatools.com/en/tools/bmr-calculator**

BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) is the calories your body burns at complete rest. This tool calculates it three ways: Mifflin-St Jeor (most accurate), Harris-Benedict (classic), and Katch-McArdle (for the body-composition nerds).

This is the foundation number for everything else on this list. If you're serious about nutrition planning, start here.

**Use it when:** You're calculating meal plans or cutting/bulking cycles.

## 5. Sleep Cycle Calculator
**https://elysiatools.com/en/tools/sleep-cycle-calculator**

Sleep happens in 90-minute cycles. Waking up mid-cycle = groggy. Waking up at the end = refreshed. This tool helps you plan for the latter.

Two modes:
- **"If I sleep now"** → gives you optimal wake-up times based on 4, 5, or 6 complete cycles
- **"If I wake at..."** → gives you optimal bedtimes

Includes a "time to fall asleep" setting (defaults to 14 minutes—realistic).

**Use it when:** You need to set an alarm that won't make you feel worse.
