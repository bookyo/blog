# 7 Free Image Format Converters for Blazing-Fast Websites

A 5MB hero image can kill your bounce rate before visitors even scroll. The solution isn't better hosting — it's smarter image formats.

WebP delivers **25–35% smaller files** than JPEG at equivalent quality. AVIF goes further, squeezing out **50% more** than WebP. Yet most sites still serve legacy formats from 1992. These seven free tools bridge that gap instantly.

## 3. PNG to WebP Converter

This converter handles alpha transparency seamlessly and gives you quality, lossless, and metadata controls. Smart subsampling is enabled by default for better compression without manual tuning.

**Best for:** UI screenshots, app mockups, logos with transparent backgrounds, and any PNG-heavy design asset.

## Why This Matters Right Now

Google's Core Web Vitals treat image weight as a first-class signal. Largest Contentful Paint (LCP) thresholds are strict, and mobile users on slower connections feel the pain of oversized images most acutely. Format conversion is a zero-compromise optimization — you get smaller files *and* better quality in many cases.

The average webpage is **2.4MB**, and images make up roughly 50–80% of that. Cutting image weight by 30–50% isn't marginal improvement — it's the difference between a passing and failing LCP score.
