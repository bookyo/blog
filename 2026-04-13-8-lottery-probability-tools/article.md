## 7. Combination Calculator — The Math Behind "How Many Ways?"

"How many ways can you arrange 5 cards from a 52-card deck?" That's C(52,5) = 2,598,960. This tool calculates it.

It handles two types:

- **Without repetition** — C(n,r) = n! / (r!(n-r)!) — choose r items from n distinct items, no duplicates
- **With repetition** — C'(n,r) = (n+r-1)! / (r!(n-1)!) — allows duplicates

It shows the full calculation steps, the Pascal's triangle position, scientific notation for large results, and real-world examples for each type.

Use it when: Designing a lottery, calculating handshake counts, or solving any combinatorics problem.
