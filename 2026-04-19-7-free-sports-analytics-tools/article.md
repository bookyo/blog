## 1. Cricket Over Rate Calculator

**What it does:** Calculates your bowling over rate and checks it against ICC minimum requirements for T20, ODI, and Test formats.

In cricket, "over rate" measures how fast a bowling team completes their overs. ICC mandates a minimum of 14.5 overs per hour in limited overs cricket. This tool tells you exactly where you stand.

Input your overs bowled, minutes played, and match format. The tool returns your current overs-per-hour rate, whether you meet the requirement, how many overs you're short (if any), and a plain-English recommendation.

## 3. Bowling Score Calculator

**What it does:** Computes ten-pin bowling scores with full frame-by-frame breakdown, including strikes, spares, and open frames.

Bowling's scoring system trips up most people — strikes are worth 10 plus the next two rolls, spares are worth 10 plus the next roll. This tool handles all of it, including the 10th frame where you can roll up to three balls.

It outputs per-frame scores, cumulative scores, and a count of strikes vs. spares vs. open frames.

**Use it for:** Casual league tracking, bowling app development, or settling arguments at the alley.

## 7. One Rep Max Calculator

**What it does:** Estimates your one-repetition maximum (1RM) from submaximal loads using five different formulas.
